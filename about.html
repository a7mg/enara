About Us

Enara is an innovative e-learning platform for neurodiverse children, created by CognitiveBotics, a leading specialized digital health-tech solutions provider headquartered in India. We offer AI-driven early, primary, and vocational interventions for neurodivergent children and late learners. Seamlessly connecting centers, schools, and therapists with parents, caregivers, and children, we have adopted a data-driven and evidence-based approach to create an impact for all parties.

Vision

We envision a world where neurodiversity is celebrated as a superpower, and where every neurodiverse child, their family, and caregivers have access to the tools and support they need to integrate into and thrive in society.

Mission

We leverage technology as a tool, coupled with specialist expertise, to intervene early, often, and effectively, changing the course of neurodiverse lives and their families.
