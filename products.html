Products & Features

Enara streamlines therapists’ tasks in treating children with a diverse range of digital tools that offer individualized learning plans and enhance communication with parents and caregivers. This not only saves time but also promotes greater parental involvement and fosters stronger relationships. Additionally, tailored digital resources minimize data collection and paperwork.For parents, Enara simplifies creating and updating their child’s treatment plan, saving time and stress while ensuring measurable impact. Daily learning sessions on our Parent Portal complement therapy sessions, promoting faster progress through remote access and cost-effective care.

Customized Learning

This AI-based storytelling feature stimulates language development, enhances comprehension skills, and teaches specific social skills engagingly. Through interactive storytelling, children embark on exciting adventures, explore diverse worlds, and learn valuable life lessons.

Parent Tutorial and Child Activities

Accessible instructional videos provide parents with practical guidance and strategies. From managing challenging behaviors to improving communication skills, these tutorials help parents play an active role in their child’s growth and social development.​These videos enhance independent engagement in child activities, making learning fun and interactive. From educational games to sensory activities, they stimulate curiosity, creativity, and exploration, fostering a love for learning in children.​

Reporting

It equips parents with comprehensive insights into their child’s progress and achievements. Through visualized data, goal tracking, and actionable recommendations, parents can easily monitor their child’s development, set meaningful goals, and collaborate effectively with therapists and educators.​

AI & Animated Games

Immersive AI-powered games help children achieve learning objectives such as communication and cognitive skills, problem-solving abilities, and social-emotional development in a captivating and dynamic way.​

Journaling

Assists parents in documenting and reflecting on their child’s responses to tailored questions. By providing a structured format for daily reflections, it helps parents identify patterns, track progress, and make informed decisions about their child’s routines and development.

Therapist Platform Portal 

Simplifies data collection and record-keeping, fostering seamless collaboration between therapists and parents. It enables therapists to input and monitor progress, share insights, and coordinate strategies with parents, ensuring a unified approach to intervention.​
