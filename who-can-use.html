Who Can Use Enara?

Centers

Traditional therapy settings face challenges due to the lack of customizable learning plans and assistive technology, making it difficult to address the diverse needs of neurodiverse children. Enara’s evidence-based treatment solutions for centers create individualized learning plans using artificial intelligence.

Enara leverages AI for gaze detection, gesture tracking, voice recognition, and an engaging rewarding system to reinforce children’s learning progress. Our platform incorporates a gamified, secure, and interactive environment, enabling parents and therapists to assess children’s progress in real-time, whether they are at home, in therapy centers, or at school.

  • Provide evidence-based treatment
  • Automate the children’s performance data collection and analysis
  • Ensure frequent communication with parents
  • Individualized learning plans
  • Reduce time spent on administrative tasks
  • Help to take on more children and provide advanced care
  • Deliver measurable impact

Therapists

Therapists struggle with accurately measuring children’s progress and communicating with all caregivers in a unified digital platform. They also encounter difficulties in customizing treatment plans to meet the specific needs of each child and spend an excessive amount of time on administrative duties.

Enara, with the help of AI, enables therapists to create learning programs tailored to each child’s unique needs and developmental stage. These programs boost children’s communication, motor, social, and academic skills. We also enhance therapy sessions by simplifying progress tracking and data analysis, integrating AI and animated games, and improving communication with parents.

  • Supplement regular sessions for faster improvement
  • Access adaptive AI tools to improve therapy outcome
  • Ensure frequent communication with parents
  • Individualized learning plans
  • Reduce documentation and administrative tasks
  • Use tech-based treatment plans to aid caregivers
  • Deliver measurable impact

Parents

Parenting special-needs children presents distinct challenges, including managing resistance to therapy, limited access to monitoring therapy progress, and the high costs of therapy sessions.

Our Parent Platform includes tutorials, activity videos, AI-powered games, and progress monitoring, developed by therapists and tech specialists to simplify caregiving. Connected to the Therapist Platform, it allows parents to access therapy session notes and set daily home learning sessions. This provides remote access and cost-effective care, keeping parents actively involved in their child’s development.

  • Facilitates a comfortable home learning environment
  • Enhance benefits from therapy sessions
  • Ensure frequent communication with parents
  • Individualized learning plans
  • Reduce documentation and administrative tasks
  • Use tech-based treatment plans to aid caregivers
  • Deliver measurable impact

Children

Children often show slow progress and reluctance to treatment in traditional therapy settings. Enara aims to change this with interactive AI and animated games that recognize and respond to voice, gaze and targeted movement, encouraging active participation. Through our fun and interactive activities, we address learning gaps and help children develop confidence and independence.

Our personalized plans help children overcome learning obstacles and unlock their full potential within a nurturing environment. By transforming therapy into an enjoyable and dynamic experience, Enara helps them reach their learning milestones at the appropriate developmental stages.
